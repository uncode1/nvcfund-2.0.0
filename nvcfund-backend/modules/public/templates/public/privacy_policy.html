Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2025

NVC Banking Platform is committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and financial information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, address, date of birth)
  • Financial information required for banking services
  • Transaction data and account activity
  • Communication records and customer service interactions
  • Technical information for security and platform optimization

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide secure banking and financial services
  • Process transactions and maintain account records
  • Comply with regulatory and legal requirements
  • Enhance security and fraud protection
  • Improve our services and customer experience

Information Security

We implement industry-leading security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Multi-factor authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and compliance monitoring
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring
  • Employee training on privacy and security protocols

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information only when:

  • Required by law or regulatory authorities
  • Necessary for transaction processing (with your consent)
  • Essential for fraud prevention and security
  • Authorized by you for specific services

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Control marketing communications preferences
  • Request account closure and data deletion (subject to legal requirements)
  • File complaints with relevant privacy authorities

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Financial records are maintained according to banking regulations and industry standards.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@nvcfund.com
  • Phone: 1-800-NVC-BANK
  • Mail: NVC Banking Platform Privacy Office

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated to customers through secure channels.
